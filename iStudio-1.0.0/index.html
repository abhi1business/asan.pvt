Why Choose Asan Windows

Discover the superior performance and quality that sets our window systems apart from traditional solutions

48% More Energy Efficient

Asan Inline Window System is 48% more energy efficient than traditional thermally broken aluminium, significantly reducing your energy costs and carbon footprint.

Superior Thermal Performance

With R-values up to 0.91, our uPVC frames provide exceptional thermal insulation, reducing heat loss by 50% and heat gain by 80% compared to standard windows.

Enhanced Security

Multi-point locking systems and internal beading provide much increased resistance to unwanted intrusion, ensuring your peace of mind and safety.

Water & Air Tight

Welded corner connections prevent leakage, while double co-extruded rubber gaskets ensure superior air-tightness, retaining warmth and preventing drafts.

Eco-Friendly & Recyclable

Our uPVC contains up to 55% recycled content and is 100% recyclable at end of life. Non-toxic, stable compound with no harmful off-gassing.

Fire Retardant

Asan uPVC is fire retardant and will not support combustion, meeting stringent safety standards with classification B-E, s3, d0 according to DIN EN 13501-1.
